Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams (FIRST)

An international organization that facilitates collaboration among computer security incident response teams (CSIRTs).

Description

The Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams (FIRST) is a global association of computer security incident response teams (CSIRTs) that aims to foster cooperation and collaboration among its members. Founded in 1990, FIRST provides a platform for sharing knowledge, experiences, and best practices in incident response and cybersecurity. The organization holds annual conferences, workshops, and training sessions, which help enhance the skills of incident response professionals. FIRST also develops guidelines and tools to assist CSIRTs in managing security incidents effectively. By promoting collaboration across various sectors, including government, academia, and private industry, FIRST helps improve the overall security posture of organizations worldwide. Its members work together to respond to incidents, share threat intelligence, and develop strategies for proactive security measures. This cooperative approach is vital in an increasingly complex and interconnected digital landscape, where cyber threats are pervasive and evolving.

Examples

  • Participation in FIRST annual conferences for knowledge sharing.
  • Collaboration on incident response scenarios and best practices among member teams.

Additional Information

  • FIRST has over 500 member teams from various sectors globally.
  • The organization also publishes resources and guidelines that are publicly accessible.
